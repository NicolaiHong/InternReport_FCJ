- Build and secure modern architectures such as serverless SPAs with authentication and performance tracing.
- Explore AI, storage, and content delivery services including Amazon Polly, Rekognition, Lex, S3, and CloudFront.
- Monitor resources using CloudWatch dashboards across different OS platforms and prepare for the weekly assessment.
